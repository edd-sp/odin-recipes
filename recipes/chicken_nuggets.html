Chicken Nuggets

Chicken nuggets are a popular fast food item made of small pieces of deboned chicken meat that are breaded or battered, then deep-fried or baked.

This is a version with an Italian touch, these chicken nuggets are delicious as an appetizer for anyone!.

Ingredients

  • 3 skinless and boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 cup melted butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (Approx. 200°C).
  2. Cut the chicken breasts into nuggets (1 1/2-inch pieces).
  3. Mix the bread crumbs, Parmesan, basil, thyme, and salt together well in a medium bowl.
  4. Dip chicken pieces into the melted butter first, then coat with the bread crumb mixture and place them on a tray with baking paper.
  5. Bake nuggets in the preheated oven until browned and chicken is cooked through, about 20 minutes.

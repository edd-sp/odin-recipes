Spaghetti alla Carbonara

A classic delicious and exquisite Italian dish that is also very easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 pound Guanciale (you can substitute it with bacon or pancetta)
  • 1 package (16 ounce) spaghetti
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 10 tablespoons grated Pecorino Romano (you can substitute it with Parmesan)
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the pork in a pan with the olive oil until browned and crispy, then drain on paper towels.
  2. Whisk the egg and yolks with half of the cheese, and some pepper in a bowl until smooth.
  3. Boil the spaghetti in salted water. Drain and cook in the pan with some of the drained oil.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the pasta, stirring quickly, until creamy.
  5. Place on a plate and stir in the pork, then top with the remaining cheese and more black pepper.
